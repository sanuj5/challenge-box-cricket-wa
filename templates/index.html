About CCA

At Challenge Cricket Academy, our mission is to cultivate accomplished cricketers by offering young, aspiring talents the essential skills, top-notch facilities, and expert training from professional coaches. Employing a

science-backed methodology, our teaching approach is tailored to extract the optimum potential from individuals across various age groups and genders.


We create a conducive environment for the holistic development of physical, mental, and social skills, placing a significant emphasis on fostering the highest level of fitness in each participant for sustained success in the

long term.


Pathik Prajapati

Head Coach of CCA

Coach Pathik stands as the exclusive cricket mentor in Anand, distinguished by his attainment of the BCCI Level 1 & 2 certification, accompanied by a Diploma in Sports Science from NIS Patiala, and the prestigious ICC Level 1 certification. With a rich background representing Gujarat at the National level, Pathik combines this experience with a forward-thinking vision, aspiring to cultivate the game from grassroots to professional levels, fostering the emergence of remarkable talents.


Rooted in the philosophy of nurturing players organically, Pathik refrains from intervening in their innate abilities, allowing for the genuine and uninhibited growth of each individual.

"My goal is to uncover the natural talent in young players. I concentrate on a holistic development approach to make sure cricketers are physically fit, mentally strong, and technically skilled. The aim is to assist players in excelling in all aspects of the game, enabling them to play longer, recover faster, stay focused, and perform more effectively."

-Pathik Prajapati
